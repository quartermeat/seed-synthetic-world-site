Seed a Synthetic World

Roguelite  ·  World Simulation  ·  Android

The planet is already activating.
Guide its emergence before Coherence collapses.

Each run begins mid-process. A synthetic planet is already becoming something — moisture spreading, life appearing, corruption creeping at the edges. Your job is to shape what it becomes.

Manage three major planetary tracks. Buy temporary upgrades. React to events. Watch the pixel-grid surface evolve in real time. Push your luck until Coherence fails.

Some runs become ocean worlds. Some collapse into corruption. Learn from each attempt. Unlock new seeds.
